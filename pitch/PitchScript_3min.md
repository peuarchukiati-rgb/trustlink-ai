# TrustLink AI — 3-Minute Pitch Script
**Shinhan Future's Lab · P4 · AABW 2026** · reference build v1.12
Spoken length ≈ 420 words (~3:00 at a calm pace). Beats are timed; `[…]` = stage/delivery notes.

---

## ⏱️ 0:00–0:25 — HOOK (a face, not a stat)

> **"Meet Aom.**
> She's a freelance designer in Ho Chi Minh City. Steady income, pays every bill on time, saves
> every single month. She applies for a small business loan — and the bank rejects her.
> Why? Because she has **no credit history**. To a traditional credit bureau, Aom is **invisible."**

`[พูดช้า สบตา หยุดหลัง "invisible" 1 วินาที — นี่คือ hook]`

---

## ⏱️ 0:25–0:50 — THE GAP + WHAT IT IS

> "**1.4 billion adults** are locked out of credit the same way — freelancers, gig workers, small
> businesses — not because they're risky, but because the system only knows how to read a credit
> file they don't have.
>
> So we built **TrustLink AI** — an explainable **Trust Intelligence & Underwriting** platform for
> Shinhan's Open Banking challenge. Instead of asking *'What's your credit history?'*, it asks
> ***'How do you actually manage money?'*** — reading Open Banking behaviour and trusted alternative
> data: pay slips, insurance, and spending."

`[ขึ้นชื่อ TrustLink AI พร้อมโชว์โลโก้/คอนโซล]`

---

## ⏱️ 0:50–1:40 — HOW IT WORKS + THE MONEY SHOT (live demo)

> "Here's Aom in our live system. TrustLink reads her cash flow across **six trust dimensions** —
> income stability, payment discipline, savings, and more — and produces a **Trust Score: 742 out
> of 1000. Strong tier.**
>
> The same borrower the bureau **rejected**, TrustLink can **approve** — with a responsible,
> affordability-checked offer of **45 million dong**. *That* is the whole point: turning a rejection
> into a **responsible approval** — and every number you see is **explainable**, not a black box."

`[DEMO: โหลด Aom → ชี้การ์ดผล 742/Strong → ชี้การ์ดเทียบ "Bureau: REJECT ↔ TrustLink: APPROVE" ค้างไว้ให้กรรมการเห็น]`
`[ถ้าเดโมล่ม: มีสกรีนช็อตการ์ดเทียบเป็น backup แล้วพูดต่อได้เลย]`

---

## ⏱️ 1:40–2:15 — THREE DIFFERENTIATORS

> "Three things make us different from a normal scorer.
>
> **One — fraud is an independent veto.** A high score can *never* buy back a fraud signal, so a
> well-funded fraudster can't slip through.
>
> **Two — we separate the Trust Score from a Confidence Score.** A thin file lowers our *certainty*,
> it never unfairly lowers the *borrower's* score. Missing data is not bad data.
>
> **Three — a human always approves.** AI recommends; a person decides. There is no autonomous
> approval in the system."

`[ชูนิ้ว 1–2–3 ตามจังหวะ ช่วยให้กรรมการจำได้]`

---

## ⏱️ 2:15–2:40 — GOVERNANCE + HONEST VALIDATION

> "Because this is for a bank, we built for the **regulator**: every data pull runs on **scoped,
> revocable consent** from **approved partners**, and every decision is written to a **reproducible
> audit trail**.
>
> In our validation, the engine cleanly **separates risk** — weak borrowers default about **three
> times** more than strong ones — and it catches **100%** of over-declared fraud. It's a prototype
> on synthetic data, but the **mechanism is proven and fully explainable.**"

`[ประโยค "prototype on synthetic data" พูดอย่างมั่นใจ — ความซื่อสัตย์คือจุดแข็ง ไม่ใช่จุดอ่อน]`

---

## ⏱️ 2:40–3:00 — IMPACT + CLOSE

> "TrustLink AI lets a bank **grow its loan book by including the people it used to reject —
> without taking on more risk.**
>
> We turn the **invisible into the approved.** That's financial inclusion, done responsibly.
>
> Thank you — we'd love to show you the full system."

`[ปิดด้วยประโยค "invisible into the approved" — วนกลับมาที่ Aom ตอนเปิด ให้เรื่องครบวง]`

---

## 🎯 Delivery tips
- **จังหวะ:** ~140 คำ/นาที เว้นจังหวะหลัง hook, หลังเลข 742, และก่อนประโยคปิด
- **โฟกัสจอ:** ให้กรรมการอยู่กับ **การ์ดเทียบ Bureau↔TrustLink** นานที่สุด — นั่นคือ money shot
- **ตัวเลขที่ต้องแม่น:** 742 / Strong / 45 ล้าน VND · Weak ผิดนัด ~3× Strong · fraud 100%
- **อย่าพูด:** "production-ready", "accurate/high accuracy", "SQLite", "multi-agent LLM" — เกินจริง
- **ถ้าเวลาเหลือ/ขาด:** ตัดได้ที่หัวข้อ 2:15–2:40 (governance) ให้สั้นลงก่อน โดยไม่แตะ hook/demo/close

## 🃏 Backup one-liners (เผื่อกรรมการถามแทรก)
- *ต่างจาก Plaid/Tink?* — "They pipe raw data; we're the **intelligence layer** that turns it into an explainable decision."
- *ต่างจาก Zest/Upstart?* — "They optimise a black-box score on bureau data; we're **Open-Banking-native, explainable, and audit-traceable** for a regulated bank."
- *fairness?* — "We monitor approval parity by segment; protected attributes are never in the schema."
- *ข้อมูลจริงไหม?* — "Synthetic for the prototype — it proves the mechanism; production calibrates on real outcomes."
